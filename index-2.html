Game rules

1、There is a 0.01BNB handling fee + Seps for the blind draw box
2、The game is normally connected to GAS and there is no handling fee
3、Withdrawal charges a one-time handling fee of 0.01BNB per transaction. There is no lock-up period to withdraw Seps at any time.
4、Repair a NFT 0.001BNB
5、Upgrade a 25Seps random increase win rate, win rate 1%-3%
(50% of the BNB handling fee is used to add the liquidity pool, 30% of the Seps repurchase to the reward pool, and 20% of the platform operation, maintenance and development fee)
